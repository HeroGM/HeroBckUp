Background
Celenthea is a Tiefling that was created along with several others in Glantri to be a slave race to one of the Princes. Decades ago they were able to excape into the Broken Lands and under and survive. Tired of living as a captitve in just another prison she decided to go to the surface world and explore, maybe find a home for her people that
they could live in without worrying about how they were living.
Personality
Still young compared to most of her friends and family, she can be a bit hot headed and tends to jump in where cooler heads were to wait. One of the big reasons she is on
the surface world exploring is she got up one day, was fed up with everything and left. No plan, no help, no real resources.
